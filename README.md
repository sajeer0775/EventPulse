# EventPulse | Global Tactical Command Center

**EventPulse** is a high-fidelity planetary intelligence platform designed for global situational awareness. It aggregates real-time telemetry from NASA EONET, USGS, and OpenSky to monitor natural hazards, seismic activity, and global transportation grids. Integrated with GenAI, it generates cinematic orbital simulations and tactical briefings, providing a mission-critical command center directly in your browser.

## 📡 Platform Intelligence

EventPulse leverages advanced Generative AI and real-time data streams:

*   **Planetary Hazards (NASA EONET)**: Real-time tracking of wildfires, storms, volcanoes, and floods.
*   **Seismic Grid (USGS)**: Live monitoring of global earthquake clusters.
*   **Neural Simulations (GenAI)**: High-resolution orbital visual simulations of active hazards.
*   **Tactical Video Core (Veo 3.0)**: AI-driven cinematic video generation.
*   **Aviation Radar (OpenSky)**: Live transponder tracking of global flight traffic.
*   **Maritime AIS (VesselFinder)**: Real-time monitoring of international shipping lanes.
*   **Market Volatility (Finnhub)**: Correlation of market indices against environmental risks.
